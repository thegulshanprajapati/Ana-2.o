Ankita Sharma — Ek Lamha, Ek Safar

Timeline
7 August 2002 — Khagaria, Bihar mein paida hui — ek kahani jo hamesha chalti rahegi.
27 July 2022 — Hamari dosti ki shuruaat.
2023 — Electrical Engineering diploma complete — ek chhoti si jeet, par dil se badi.

Ankita Sharma, ek ladki jiska bachpan struggles se bhara tha. Financial problems, society ka pressure aur apne sapno ko poora karne ki lagan ne usse alag banaya. Uski aankhon mein ek sharmili si chamak thi, par andar ek dumdaar jazba chhupa hua tha.

Bachpan aur Jung

Chhoti si zindagi mein bade bade imtihaan the. Ghar ki pareshaniyan usse kabhi rok nahi paayi. Usne apna dard hamesha chhupaya, par padhai aur sapne ko apna sabse bada weapon banaya. Diary uska ek khaas dost thi — phone kho jaane ke baad bhi usne har zaroori number wahan likh rakha tha. Yeh uski practicality aur intelligence ka saboot tha.

Sapne, Sankalp aur Diploma

Jahan ladkiyon ke liye electrical engineering mushkil lagti thi, wahan usne challenge accept kiya. Usne kaha, “Main kar sakti hoon,” aur 2023 mein apna diploma complete karke apne sapne ke raaste ko aur roshan kar diya.

Hamari Mulaqat — 27 July 2022

Woh din yaadgar tha. Jab hum mile toh mujhe laga ki yeh ladki alag hai — thodi introvert, par ek anjaani si strength thi usmein. Tabhi maine usey pyaar se ek naam diya — “Billi.”

Uske baad se meri zindagi mein woh sirf Ankuu ban gayi ❤️. Dheere-dheere baatein badhi, hansna-mazaak hua aur dosti ek nayi raah par chal padi.

Dooriyan, Galatfahmiyan aur Galtiyaan

Zindagi kabhi simple nahi hoti. Dheere-dheere misunderstandings hui. Ankuu job ke liye Neemrana chali gayi, aur main Kolkata. Wahan main ek galti mein fas gaya — kisi aur rishton mein ulajh kar apne asli dost se door ho gaya. Jab reality saamne aayi, toh samajh aaya ki main kitna bura faisla le chuka tha.

Wahi Call — Wahi Ehsaas

Jab Ankuu ka phone khoya, usne apne diary ke numbers se apne doston ko call kiya. Ek call meri taraf bhi aayi, lekin us waqt maine use block kar rakha tha. Jab mujhe baad mein pata chala, toh main andar se toot gaya. Usne kabhi blame nahi kiya, bas sach keh diya. Us sach mein ek ehsaas chhupa tha jo mujhe zindagi bhar yaad rahega.

Seekhna, Maafi aur Wapas Aana

Galti ko samajhna aur sudharna sabse badi jeet hoti hai. Maine apni zindagi se wo uljhan nikal di aur asli dosti ko fir se apnaya. Dheere-dheere Ankuu ne mujhe wapas accept kiya, aur humne phir se woh hasi-mazaak, woh raaton ki baatein jeeni shuru ki.

Aaj Ki Ankuu — Apni Zindagi Ki Malika

Aaj Ankuu apne sapno ko apne haathon mein lekar jeeti hai. Uski sharmili si muskaan ab confidence mein badal gayi hai. Jo ladki kabhi society ke pressure ke samne dar jaati thi, aaj apni khud ki duniya bana rahi hai. Apne struggles ko apni taqat mein badal kar, Ankuu ek inspiration ban chuki hai.

Kuch Chhote Pal — Jo Yaad Rehte Hain

Chai ki wo dukaan jahan humne pehli baar hasi-mazaak kiya.

Exam ke results ka intezaar karte hue ki gayi raat ki baatein.

Uski diary ke woh words: “Main kabhi apne sapno se compromise nahi karungi.”

Aur ek chhoti si “thank you” jo usne dil se bola tha, jo mere liye sabse bada reward tha.

Aakhri Alfaaz — Dil Se

Yeh kahani sirf ek dost ki nahi, ek inspiration ki bhi hai. Har rishte mein utar-chadhav aate hain, par asli dosti wahi hai jo waqt ke saath aur gehri ho.

“Ankuu, tu meri zindagi ka woh hissa hai jo hamesha mere saath rahega. Tere sapne, teri muskaan aur teri himmat mujhe hamesha inspire karte rahenge. Apni duniya apne andaaz mein jeeti rehna — kyunki tu ussi layak hai.” ❤️
